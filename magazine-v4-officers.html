Welcome to Glyn Dwfn

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), a non-profit educational organization dedicated to researching and recreating the arts, skills, and traditions of pre-17th century Europe.

Our officers work tirelessly to organize events, maintain safety standards, manage finances, and ensure that everyone has a positive experience in the SCA.

Lord Thomas the Bold

All Officers

Our officers serve in various roles to keep the barony running smoothly. From organizing events to managing finances, each officer plays a crucial part in our community.

  • Minister of Arts & Sciences - Coordinates workshops and competitions
  • Exchequer - Manages barony finances and reporting
  • Chronicler - Publishes newsletter and maintains records
  • Herald - Conducts court and assists with heraldry
  • Web Minister - Maintains website and social media
