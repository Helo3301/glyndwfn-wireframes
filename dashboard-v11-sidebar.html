Welcome to the Barony of Glyn Dwfn

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), dedicated to researching and recreating pre-17th century European culture.

Join us for weekly practices, monthly events, and kingdom-wide tournaments. From armored combat to period arts, there's something for everyone!
