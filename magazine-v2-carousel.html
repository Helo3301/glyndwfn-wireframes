Welcome to Glyn Dwfn

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), a non-profit educational organization dedicated to researching and recreating the arts, skills, and traditions of pre-17th century Europe.

Our barony serves central Kentucky, bringing together people passionate about history, combat sports, period arts, and medieval culture.

Getting Started

New to the SCA? No problem! We welcome newcomers of all ages and backgrounds. Come to one of our weekly practices or monthly events to meet our members, try on armor, and learn about what we do.

Loaner gear is available for combat activities, and our artisans are always happy to share their knowledge and help you discover your medieval passion.
