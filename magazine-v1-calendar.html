Welcome to Glyn Dwfn

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), a non-profit educational organization dedicated to researching and recreating the arts, skills, and traditions of pre-17th century Europe.

Our barony serves central Kentucky, bringing together people passionate about history, combat sports, period arts, and medieval culture. Whether you're a seasoned member or completely new to the SCA, you'll find a welcoming home here.

Combat Sports

Train in armored combat, rapier fighting, and archery with experienced instructors. We provide loaner equipment for beginners and maintain a supportive environment for fighters of all skill levels.

Arts & Sciences

Explore calligraphy, illumination, brewing, cooking, leatherwork, sewing, metalwork, and countless other medieval arts. Share your research, learn new skills, and compete in arts competitions.
