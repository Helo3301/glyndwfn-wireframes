About Our Barony

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), a non-profit educational organization dedicated to researching and recreating the arts, skills, and traditions of pre-17th century Europe.

We meet regularly for practices, workshops, and social gatherings. Whether you're interested in martial activities, arts and sciences, or simply enjoying the company of like-minded individuals, you'll find a welcoming community here.
