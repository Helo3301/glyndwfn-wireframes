About Our Barony

The Barony of Glyn Dwfn is part of the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA), a non-profit educational organization dedicated to researching and recreating the arts, skills, and traditions of pre-17th century Europe.

We meet regularly for combat practices, arts workshops, and social gatherings. Whether you're drawn to the thrill of armored combat, the precision of period crafts, or the warmth of medieval fellowship, you'll find your place here.
